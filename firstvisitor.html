First Time Visitors

Welcome to Taniti! If this is your first time visiting our beautiful island, we have some tips to help you make the most of your trip.

1. Pack Light

As a tropical island, Taniti is warm year-round. Be sure to pack light, breathable clothing, sunscreen, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun. Don't forget your swimsuit and beach towel for relaxing on the beach!

2. Stay Hydrated

The warm weather in Taniti can be dehydrating, so be sure to drink plenty of water throughout the day. You can also enjoy fresh coconut water, tropical fruit juices, and other refreshing beverages to stay hydrated. The legal drinking age here is 18, and alcohol stops being sold between midnight and 9 AM.

3. Explore the Island

There is so much to see and do on Taniti, from beautiful beaches to lush rainforests. Be sure to explore the island and take in the natural beauty of the landscape. You can hike, bike, or take a boat tour to see the sights.

4. Try the Local Cuisine

Taniti is known for its delicious food, from fresh seafood to tropical fruits. Be sure to try the local cuisine, including traditional island dishes and international fare. You can dine at beachside restaurants, local markets, and street food stalls to sample the flavors of Taniti.

5. Relax and Unwind

One of the best things about Taniti is the laid-back atmosphere and beautiful scenery. Take some time to relax and unwind on the beach, in a hammock, or at a spa. You can also enjoy a sunset cruise, beachside yoga class, or massage to rejuvenate your body and mind.
